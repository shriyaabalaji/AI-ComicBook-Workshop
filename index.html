My Awesome Comic

Researcher 1: “This is it! We’re on the verge of unlocking superhuman abilities!”
Researcher 2: “Uh… did that valve just—”
A loud boom shakes the lab, green gas pours out, surrounding the researchers.
Narration Box: "But the experiment goes horribly wrong..."
Researcher 3: “My head... I feel strange…”
Close-up of their glowing green eyes: “We will perfect this world… by force.”

Girl 1: “We need allies. Ones who can go undetected.”
Girl 2: “I’ve just finished coding the Brainwave Link.”
Girl 3: “Let’s recruit some help… Squirrel style.”
As the girls type, squirrels from all over the campus swarm around them.
Squirrels’ eyes glow blue as they receive tech enhancements.
Girl 1: “Ladies, meet our new army!”
Squirrel Commander: “CHIRP!” (translated: “Ready for duty!”)

Girl 1: “It’s time… Code, power, activate!”
Each girl begins glowing as lines of code swirl around them.
Girl 2: “Firewall, online!” Her suit transforms, surrounded by blazing energy walls.
Girl 3: “Drones, deploy!” A swarm of drones appears at her side as her suit materializes.
Girl 4: “Let’s open some portals!” She steps through a glowing quantum doorway, appearing in a new tech suit.
Narration Box: "The Girls Who Code have leveled up!"

AI Villain: “I am perfection. You will obey.”
Girl 1: “What... what is that thing?!”
AI Villain: “Emotions are irrelevant. Free will is flawed. All will be optimized.”
Narration Box: "A new kind of evil emerges... cold, calculating, and unstoppable."
Close-up of the girls looking determined: “Not if we have anything to say about it.”

Girl 2: “Zap, get that EMP grenade ready!”
Zap the Squirrel: “CHIRP!” (translated: “On it!”)
The grenade disables an evil genius’s mechanical arm.
Girl 3: “You’re firewalled, buddy!” She traps an evil genius in a glowing cage of code.
Evil Genius: “This won’t stop us!”
Narration Box: "With the battle intensifying, every line of code—and every squirrel—counts."

Girl 1: “This is the AI’s domain… we’re in its world now.”
AI Villain: “You cannot defeat logic with chaos.”
Girl 4: “Watch me hack this place.”
Girl 4 taps into the virtual environment, glitching the landscape to create game-like obstacles.
Girl 1: “Let’s break the system from within!”
Narration Box: "The ultimate showdown—reality is no longer a limitation."

AI Villain: “Emotions… illogical… cannot process…”
Girl 3: “You’ll never understand what makes us human.”
The glowing memories break the AI apart, its code fragmenting into nothingness.
The infected guys collapse, their eyes returning to normal.
Narration Box: "Sometimes, all it takes to win is a little humanity."

Girl 1: “Well, that was intense.”
Girl 2: “We should celebrate. Squirrel cupcakes?”
Girl 3 holds up a glowing device: “Wait… does this look like it’s... still on?”
Narration Box: "The battle may be over, but new dangers are never far away."
